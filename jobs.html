Recruitment Process:

The Infosys recruitment process for system Engineer role consists of 3 rounds in total.
They are as follows:

• Online Test (This includes Reasoning Ability, Mathematical Ability & Logical Reasoning, Verbal Ability, Pseudocode and Puzzles)
• Technical Interview.
• HR Interview.

Eligibility Criterion

    Academic Criteria

1) A candidate must have more than 60% marks in 10th and 12th (or diploma).
2) A candidate must have a minimum of 65% marks or CGPA 6.5 in graduation.
3) A maximum of 2 years of a gap is permissible in the entire education.
4) A candidate should not have any pending backlogs at the time of appearing for Infosys selection process.

    Graduation Criteria

1) B.E / B.Tech in any discipline (CSE/ECE/IT/CIVIL/ME/EEE/Automobile/Aeronautical etc.)
2) Engineering candidates who have passed out in the year 2020 are eligible to apply.
3) M.Sc graduates in Computer Science/ Information technology can apply.
4) MCA graduates are also eligible to apply

    Documents required:

1) All original mark sheets related to SSC/CBSC (10th Class), Intermediate(12th Standard) and Graduation are required
to be produced at the time of interview. Also, please carry a copy of set of all documents at the time of interview.
2) A copy of candidate's updated resume.
3) Passport size photographs.
4) An original identification proof issued by the government of India (Aadhar Card, Pan Card, Voter id, Passport etc.)

    Infosys Technical Interview Round

You will be expected to have a strong knowledge of C, C++, and JAVA.
Basic Technical questions from C, Java, SQL etc. will be asked. You may be asked:
•To find the output of the given program.
•To write syntax and programs.
•Regarding academics.
•Your role in the project.
•Your internships.
•Questions circling on your area of interests.
